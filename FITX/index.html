ABOUT US

FITX is the principal strength and conditioning program for people of all shapes and sizes, from the highly specialized combat warrior to the grandmother trying to build enough strength to pick herself up after a fall. This is a fitness program that is, by design, broad, general and inclusive. Our franchise is a limited liability company located in Bandra, Mumbai.
